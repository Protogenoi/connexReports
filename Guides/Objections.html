Objections

Remember the rule of three, and handle client objections.

I'm busy!

Yeah I understand your busy, however this review is very brief and we will be able to give you an unbiased review almost straight away.

I'm happy with existing provider!

Yeah I understand, however just for your re-assurance we aren’t an insurance company but we complete the reviews for all the major insurers.

Do you have my policy information?

Just to re-assure you, we don’t hold information like that as we’re not your insurance company and we have to complete unbiased review.

I have an IFA!

Yeah that’s no problem, has your advisor reviewed it in the past 18 months? (If no) It is highly recommended you should have it looked at in that time because of changes in circumstances, we are a review service and able to complete an unbiased review.

Not interested!

I can understand what you are saying, however this service is very important. We need to check your policy is up to date.

Discuss it with partner

It’s important you don’t put off planning for your financial future now, otherwise it might be something you mean to do but never get round to. Just as an indication, cover starts from only £6 per month and some cover is better than nothing. Could you really not afford to spend the equivalent of at least 20p a day on having some cover to help in times of need? Value of parent Plus if you have a family, the additional work you do around the home and looking after the children works out to the value of around £30,000 for a mum and £21,300 for a Dad per year. How would you pay to replace this help if you or your partner died or were unable to undertake these tasks. Your actual premium will depend on your individual circumstances.
